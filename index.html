About Me

Hello! Here's a quick background on me. I previously worked in marketing as a graphic designer and copywriter but went through two layoffs in two years during the Great Recession and decided to completely change careers. I did a Ruby on Rails bootcamp seven years ago which was not very good. I honestly never really figured out how to program Ruby during that time, but it did open doors and got me into a tech career as a web support technician for a newspaper company.

I did that for a year before being recruited by an adtech company where I’ve worked for the past five years as a sales engineer. My position has evolved quite a bit in those five years and I now see a big need for me to really learn programming so I can build tools for my team and query our database more effectively and efficiently. Not to mention I feel like I’ve plateaued in my position and want to do bigger things and advance in my career. So, I decided to take a step back and do a fullstack bootcamp to fill in the gaps in my knowledge and build a really strong foundation in programming.
