About Ann

Ann Karpinski grew up in Little Falls, MN and attended the University of Minnesota – Twin Cities, earning a B.S. degree in Journalism and Fisheries & Wildlife Management. She spent the next 10 years working in communications for several environmental non-profit organizations in Minnesota, Washington DC, and Colorado. During this time, she studied graphic design and photography to compliment her copywriting skills. After a year of working as a greeting card verse writer (because, why not?!) she decided to completely shake things up and attend a Ruby on Rails bootcamp to help get her foot in the door in the tech industry.

Her first job in tech was as a web support specialist for a newspaper company in Denver. After a year there, she was recruited by adtech company SpotX in 2015 as a technical operations engineer. A little over a year later she was promoted to solutions engineer and works in that role today as a conduit between business operations and engineering. She enjoys finding tech solutions that will help her clients succeed in programmatic advertising. In a curious twist of fate, she now finds herself working with the very broadcasters, publishers, and media companies she admired growing up and that prompted her to pursue a journalism degree. Transitioning to a tech career has opened up exciting doors for her.

With the exception of the Ruby on Rails bootcamp, Ann has not received a formal education in tech. So in March 2020, she decided to undertake the University of Denver’s full-stack software development bootcamp to fill in gaps in her knowledge and to try on the shoes of a software developer. She expects to graduate in September 2020.
